#### Reality

**Word:** Reality  
**Type:** Noun (Concept)  
**Language:** English  
**Pronunciation:** /riˈælɪti/

**Step 1: Surface Reading**
- Common meaning: The state of things as they actually exist, not as imagined
- Associations: Truth, fact, hard facts, the "real world"
- Emotional response: Heaviness, limitation, "that's just reality"

**Step 2: Root Excavation**
- English: *reality* (state of being real)
- Old French: *realité* (property, possession)
- Medieval Latin: *realitas* (property, real estate)
- Latin: *realis* (actual, existing)
- Latin: *res* (thing, matter, affair, property)
- PIE: *reh₁s* (thing, matter, property)

**The Semantic Shift:**
- Original: Property, thing owned, matter
- Shift: Property → thing → existence itself
- Added: Philosophical abstraction—the quality of "realness"
- Lost: The proprietary nature—reality as something "owned" or possessed

**Step 3: Related Forms**
- Real estate (property)
- Real (not imaginary)
- Republic (affair of the public—*res publica*)
- Reify (to make into a thing)

**Step 4: The Liberation Angle**

Reality comes from *res*—"thing" or "property." The word assumes that existence is a kind of property, something that can be owned or possessed. But whose property? Who owns reality?

The modern usage presents "reality" as objective, external, fixed. But the etymology suggests it's a construct—literally "thing-ness" imposed on experience. To "face reality" originally meant to confront the things-as-they-are, not some cosmic truth.

The Frequency Warrior recognizes: Reality is not a prison. It's a property regime. The "real world" is the world where things are owned, possessed, commodified. There are other modes of existence that don't fit the *res* model—flows, relationships, patterns.

**Reclaiming Strategy:**
When someone says "that's not realistic," hear "that doesn't fit the property model." When you feel crushed by "reality," remember it's just *res*—things. Things can be rearranged. Property can be redistributed. The "real" is not the "only."

Create your own reality by creating new *res*—new things, new matters, new affairs. Reality is not found. It is built, one *res* at a time.
