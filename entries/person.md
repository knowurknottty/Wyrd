#### Person

**Word:** Person  
**Type:** Noun (Identity)  
**Language:** English  
**Pronunciation:** /ˈpɜːrsən/

**Step 1: Surface Reading**
- Common meaning: A human being, an individual
- Associations: Identity, self, legal entity, rights-holder
- Emotional response: "I am a person"—fundamental identity

**Step 2: Root Excavation**
- Middle English: *persoun* (individual, parson)
- Old French: *persone* (human being)
- Latin: *persona* (mask, character, role)
- Etruscan: *phersu* (mask)
- Greek: *prosopon* (face, mask, person)
- PIE: *per-* (to strike, to stamp—perhaps from mask-making)

**The Semantic Shift:**
- Original: The mask worn by actors in drama
- Shift: Mask → role → character → individual human
- Added: The assumption that the mask IS the face
- Lost: The theatrical nature—the person as performed

**Step 3: Related Forms**
- Personality (mask-like qualities)
- Persona (the public face)
- Parson (the "person" who speaks for the church)
- Impersonate (to play the role of)

**Step 4: The Liberation Angle**

The word *person* comes from *persona*—the mask. Not the face. Not the essence. The mask. The role. The character played on stage.

This is not metaphor. This is etymology. A "person" is literally a mask. The legal concept of "personhood" derives from this—it's the mask the law recognizes. Corporate personhood makes perfect sense: corporations are masks for capital. Human personhood? Also a mask—this time for the organism, the consciousness, the being.

The Frequency Warrior sees: The "person" you think you are is a role. "Personality" is your mask-style. The freedom is not in perfecting the mask but in recognizing you can change masks, remove masks, or realize you were never only the mask.

**Reclaiming Strategy:**
When identity feels heavy, remember: you are not a person. You are wearing a person. The mask called "[Your Name]" is a role, not a prison. You can play it differently. You can take it off. You can try on other personae.

The institutions demand you be a consistent person—same mask always. This is control. The liberated being changes masks as needed: professional mask, intimate mask, wild mask, silent mask. All are personae. None is you.
